Masonry Walls

How walls carry loads
50
minutes

1 Wall Behavior

Walls carry vertical loads in compression. Also resist lateral loads.

2 Openings Matter

Doors and windows interrupt load path. Load flows AROUND openings.

3 Lintels

Beams over openings that carry load to wall sections on each side.

4 Stress Check

Verify wall stress is below allowable. œÉ = P/A must be safe.

Foundation Design

Sizing foundations for your loads
50
minutes

1 Why Foundations?

Soil is weak. Foundations spread load to reduce soil pressure.

2 Bearing Capacity

Maximum pressure soil can handle. Different soils, different capacities.

3 Sizing Formula

Area = Load / Allowable Pressure. Bigger load = bigger footing.

4 Bridge Adds Load!

Your foundations must carry building weight PLUS bridge reaction!
