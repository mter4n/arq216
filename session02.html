Dead & Live Load Theory

What your tower must carry
50
minutes

1 Dead vs Live Loads

Permanent loads (self-weight) vs variable loads (people, equipment).

2 Load Factors

Why we multiply loads by safety factors. "Might work" vs "will work."

3 Load Combinations

How loads combine in the worst case. Vertical + lateral forces together.

4 Competition Loads

Vertical stacking test + lateral push test. Both must be resisted.

Material Testing Lab

Hands-on testing of popsicle sticks
50
minutes

1 Dimensions & Weight

Measure stick dimensions with calipers. Calculate density and cross-section area.

2 Bending Test

Support at two ends, load at center until failure. Record breaking load.

3 Compression Test

Stand stick vertically, apply load until buckling. Observe short vs long behavior.

4 Data Recording

Document all measurements. Calculate averages. This data goes into Robot.
