Setup & Planning

Prepare workspace and review design
50
minutes

1 Workspace Setup

Organize your station. Lay out all materials. Prepare glue and cutting area.

2 Materials Check

Count popsicle sticks (100 max). Verify all supplies present.

3 Review Design

Study your locked drawings. Identify build sequence. Plan which joints first.

4 Team Roles

Assign tasks: cutting, assembling, gluing, checking. Work parallel when possible.

üî®

Build Phase I

Foundation and main structure
50
minutes

1 Base Construction

Start from the bottom. Build a stable, level foundation.

2 Vertical Members

Erect main columns/posts. Ensure they're plumb. Check alignment.

3 First Floor

Connect horizontal members for first level. Verify squareness before glue sets.

4 Quality Connections

Good joints = strong tower. Apply glue carefully. Hold until set.

‚òï

Coffee Break I ‚Äî 20 minutes

Let glue cure. Review progress. Plan remaining work.

‚ö°

Build Phase II

Upper levels and bracing
50
minutes

1 Continue Upward

Build remaining floors. Maintain alignment. Each level must match design.

2 Add Bracing

Install diagonal members. These resist lateral loads. Critical for wind test.

3 Check Geometry

Compare to drawings. Is it matching? Robot assumed THIS geometry.

4 Reinforce Weak Points

Identify concerning joints. Add glue where needed. Stay within design.

‚úÖ

Finish & Document

Final touches and photo documentation
50
minutes

1 Final Assembly

Complete all remaining connections. Last chance for construction work.

2 Quality Inspection

Check every joint. Look for loose connections. Test stability gently.

3 Photo Documentation

Photograph from multiple angles. Document before testing. This is evidence.

4 Weigh Tower

Record final weight. Needed for efficiency calculation. Lighter = better.
