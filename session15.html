minutes

1 Inspect Your Part

2 Measure Again

Verify connection height one more time. This is your last chance!

3 Coordinate Sequence

All teams agree: what connects first? Bridge to A, then B? Or?

4 Position Models

Place buildings at correct spacing. Match the agreed bridge span.

minutes

1 First Connection
