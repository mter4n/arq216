Vertical Load Test

How much can your tower hold?
50
minutes

1 Position Tower

Place tower on testing platform. Center loading plate. Verify stability.

2 Incremental Loading

Add weights gradually. Document behavior. Watch for warning signs.

3 Load to Failure

Continue until failure. Record ultimate load. Document failure mode.

4 Record Results

Log actual capacity vs prediction. Calculate accuracy. Photo the failure.

Lateral Push Test

How does your tower resist sideways force?
50
minutes

1 Reset or New Tower

If tower survived vertical test, use same tower. Otherwise, test surviving portion.

2 Apply Lateral Force

Horizontal push at specified height. Gradual increase. Watch for sway.

3 Push to Failure

Continue until collapse or significant yielding. Record ultimate lateral capacity.

4 Calculate Scores

Efficiency = Load / Weight. Accuracy = |Prediction - Actual|. Compile results.
